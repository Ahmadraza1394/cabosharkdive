Mexico's #1 Shark Diving Company since 2015 CABO SHARK DIVE is a FIVE-STAR shark diving company, the first and only official and certified shark diving operator in Los Cabos and Baja California Sur with regular license and permit of the Port Authorities. We have been successfully and safely operating in Cabo since 2015, offering the best and safest shark diving, snorkeling, freediving, and shark swimming experience in Cabo San Lucas with great customer service and thousands of happy customers every year! We provide WORLD CLASS shark diving and snorkeling tours and expeditions with the world's most prestigious shark species and amazing marine creatures of Baja California Sur. Our professional and highly specialized crew will bring you snorkeling, swimming, diving, freediving SAFELY without cages with Silky Sharks, Blue Sharks, Mako Sharks, Smooth Hammerheads, Whale Sharks, Mantas, schools of Mobula Rays, Striped Marlin, Whales, Dolphins, and other big marine animals.

All our tours are suitable for everyone and do not require diving certification. All trips are available both as regular tours and private charters, and we also organize tailor-made expeditions. Our team consists of Marine Biologists, professional underwater photographers, PADI certified diving instructors, dive masters, professional shark handlers, and shark behavioral experts with over 16 years of experience. You are in very good hands!

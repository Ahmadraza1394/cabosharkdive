Why Cabo Shark Dive?

MEXICO'S #1 SHARK DIVING COMPANY

CABO SHARK DIVE is a FIVE-STAR shark diving company, pioneer of shark diving operations in Baja California, safely and successfully operating in Cabo San Lucas since 2015. We are the FIRST and only certified shark diving company in Cabo San Lucas and Baja California Sur, with all required licenses and permits from the Port Authorities. We are an expert team of marine biologists, professional underwater photographers, PADI certified dive instructors, sharks behavioural experts with years of experience working and diving with sharks and big marine animals worldwide. We provide UP CLOSE AND PERSONAL wildlife encounters with the most prestigious shark species and marine wildlife in the pristine waters of the Pacific Ocean and Sea of Cortez.

ECO-TOURS OPEN TO EVERYONE - 100% SAFETY RECORDS

All our tours are suitable to everyone, NO DIVING CERTIFICATION REQUIRED, no previous experience needed! Our experienced crew members are trained as Emergency First Responders (EFR) with no accidents in our tours history. We pride ourselves on exceptional customer service, creating once in a lifetime experiences for thousands of happy guests every year. Our tours are FAMILY FRIENDLY.

RESEARCH PROJECTS, EDUCATION, AND CONSERVATION

Cabo Shark Dive is deeply committed to sharks and marine wildlife conservation. During our eco-tours we collect very important information and data to understand sharks behaviour, regional trends, migratory routes or patterns in biological populations. We actively participate with an essential technical and logistical support in several research projects and scientific conservation programs such as SHARK MONITORING, SHARK TAGGING, CITIZEN SCIENCE AND EDUCATIONAL ACTIVITIES in collaboration with SHARK-LAB-CABO and MEXICO AZUL. NICE AND COMFORTABLE BOATS WITH ALL THE AMENITIES

Best sea-worthy, well maintained and comfortable boats with bathroom, plenty of shade and open deck. All boats have all safety equipment requested by the PORT AUTHORITIES: gps and radio systems, life jackets, lifesavers, first aid botiquin, fire extinguishers etc.

HIGHEST SUCCESS RATE FOR SHARKS AND MARINE LIFE ENCOUNTERS IN LOS CABOS

Our Team spent countless hours exploring the ocean and interacting with marine wildlife, to better understand the sharks behaviour and their migratory patterns. Our experience and knowledge maximize the chances of incredible sharks and marine life encounters everyday. We know the best spots to guarantee the best and safest sharks, whales and marine life encounters.

UNDERWATER PHOTOS/VIDEOS AND SNORKELING GEAR INCLUDED ON OUR TOURS

High-quality underwater photos or videos of your experience and full snorkeling gear included on our tours.

ECO-TOURS AND RESPECTFUL INTERACTIONS

We love and respect the ocean and all marine wild animals. We provide ONLY respectful encounters, we never force interactions and we respect the natural space of sharks, whales etc. to guarantee the safety of both humans and wildlife. We provide up close and personal interactions, but we never touch or harass the marine life.

SAFETY ON TOUR

The Cabo Shark Snorkeling Tour is ABSOLUTELY SAFE, we have a 100% safety record with no accidents in all our tour history. Cabo Shark Dive has the Official Authorisation and Permit of the Mexican Federal Institutions and Port Authorities for shark diving/snorkelling activities in Cabo San Lucas. Our Shark diving/snorkelling tour follows the rigorous professional global protocols of the shark diving industry, with tested and reliable safety measures. With more than 15 years of experience in shark diving operations we guarantee that you are in the best hands! The safety and comfort of our guests is a priority for us, in and out of the water. All our divers and watchers will receive careful and direct supervision during all the experience by our certified and experienced guides. Our crew members are trained as EMERGENCY FIRST RESPONDERS and EFR Instructors and they are qualified to use all medical equipment onboard and handle any kind of situation. Our boats are fully equipped with radio and gps, full Medical kits and all the safety devices required by the Port Authorities. With a constant and direct supervision of the snorkelers, we ensure safety, comfort and enjoyment during the whole experience. Our guides enter the water first and stay with you throughout, guaranteeing a safe and enjoyable experience. Just ask – we're here to help!

Mobula Rays Snorkeling Your

Here in Cabo we are blessed to have the world biggest Mobula rays aggregation. Every year from May until July, during the mating season, millions of this beautiful mini-manta rays form massive schools right outside of the Marina. Is pretty easy to spot the big school because they have the unique behaviour to jump with the full body outside the water. Once we spot them jumping we slowly get close with our boat and we jump in the water. What you will see is absolutely stunning, you will be enveloped by thousands of Mobula rays, a totally safe, once in a lifetime experience. We will put you right inside the biggest schools of rays you have never seen before! All the tours are led with expertise by the the Cabo Shark Dive Team. Mobula rays are completely harmless.
